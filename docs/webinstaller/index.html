Flipper Zero Mayhem

Flipper Zero Mayhem installer

Install the following apps to your Flipper (alternatively, read about the compilation):
  • MAYHEM_Marauder.fap
  • MAYHEM_Camera.fap
  • MAYHEM_Motion.fap
  • MAYHEM_QRcode.fap
Next, while holding pin IO0 connected to GND, press reset on the ESP32CAM and upload the firmware pressing Install. For more details on the apps check this video, or take a look of the documentation.

Note 1: If you are using your Flipper to upload the firmware, remember to go to GPIO/USB-UART Bridge. After the upload, reset the ESP32 (or unplug/replug the module).

Note 2: If you do not see the Install button below, your browser might not support this functionality.

Enjoy!
